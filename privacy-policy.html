Introduction

SportsToks ("we", "our", or "the app") is a sports card collection and trivia app for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify and improve the app experience:

  • Google Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data such as app opens, screen views, session duration, device type, operating system version, and general location (country/region). This data is anonymous and cannot identify you personally.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports and diagnostic data to help us fix bugs and improve app stability. This includes device model, OS version, and stack traces.
  • Google AdMob: Serves advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Information Stored Locally

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your card collection progress
  • Quiz scores and statistics
  • App preferences

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not collect your precise location
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or files
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics)
  • Google AdMob

Advertising

SportsToks displays banner and interstitial advertisements powered by Google AdMob. You can manage your ad preferences through your device's Google Settings.

Children's Privacy

SportsToks is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

Local app data is retained on your device until you uninstall the app or clear the app data.

Your Rights

  • Clear all local app data through Settings > Apps > SportsToks > Clear Data
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device's Google Settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time to remove all local data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.
